Health Care Hospital Colombo is an ultra-modern multi-specialty 120 bedded corporate hospital located within the ‘Hospital Zone’ of Narahenpita, Colombo. We are committed to providing compassionate and high-quality medical care of international standards with a focus on value based healthcare i.e. providing the best clinical outcomes at optimum cost. We also provide the best clinical talent of the country and supplemented it with a highly skilled and competent nursing and technical team, robust support services, a modern infrastructure,the latest medical equipment and diagnostics, with cutting edge technology and a pleasing ambiance.
